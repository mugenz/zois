THE WORLD WE LIVE IN

The world is permanently changing. We live in uncertain times.
The companies are facing many challenges:
Externally, they are confronted with topics like climate change, energy issues, digitalization and other disruptive forces.
Internally, they are confronted with challenges like cost pressure, reorganization, new business models and the new work model.
Companies need trusted advisors and business consultants to discuss the relevant implications.

TRANSFORMATION

Transformation is key to keep sustainable performance also in the future.
Speed is essential in the markets of the future, and time is running.
It’s all about the right execution. The discipline of “getting things done”.
Management resources, experience and know how are limiting factors within many organizations to effectively execute transformation projects.
Companies can cover this temporary need with an experienced Interim Manager.

MY PURPOSE - the WHY

My desire is to serve the society and to contribute for a better today and tomorrow.
The purpose of my services is to create sustainable value for companies and stakeholders.
As an impactful changemaker I aim to inspire people and to unleash their potential.

MY SERVICES - the WHAT

As a senior executive, transformation manager, business consultant or senior advisor I support industrial mid-sized companies with international setup in their business transformation.
Based on trustful communication and collaboration, I develop individuals and teams and drive the effective execution of transformation and sales growth projects.
The delivered outcomes are a higher employee engagement and customer loyalty, increased effectivity and efficiency in sales & marketing, successful integration of acquired companies and so increased competitiveness.
