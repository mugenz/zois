About Me

The FOUNDATION

Konstantin Zois was born in the “Summer of 69” and grew up in Schwaebisch Gmuend (area of Stuttgart).

He holds a Diploma in Business Administration from the University of Erlangen-Nuremberg.
Focus courses were Strategic Management, Industrial Management, Constitutional and European Law.

Holding several positions in the functions strategy, controlling, sales/business development, program/project management and working together with diverse people around the globe, helped him to become an authentic leader and a successful general manager.

In his private life he spends his time with his family and invests his time in reading articles & books about management, philosophy, politics, and soccer.
